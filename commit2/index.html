COMMIT²

Video, 42", 2020

COMMIT² is a data visualization and sonification work created by Ren Yuan. By getting the commit history of more than 600 developers on the Processing, p5.js, Processing.py and Processing for Android project on GitHub, the addition, modification, deletion, and renaming of project files by developers can be visualized and audible in a weekly update manner. The commit history is from July 2001 to November 2020.
