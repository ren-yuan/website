VINCI

Video, 2' 5", 2017

VINCI is a video created by Ren Yuan while he was the Art Adviser of VINCI. The visual of the work was generated by Ren Yuan through coding: he simulated a neural network through abstract node connection, then generated various forms based on the network structure, the whole network consists of 1950 nodes with short-range connections to each other. The music is created by Liu Yiwei.
