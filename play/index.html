PLAY

Program / Video, Real-time / 2' 14", 2016

PLAY is an audiovisual program based on coding. The visual work is created by Ren Yuan, He took a free style for his creation, and he did an exploratory experiment on different visual forms through computer programming before finally combining fragmented experiment results to form a complete work. The music is created by Hong Qile.
